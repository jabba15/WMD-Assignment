General Questions

How long does detailing take?

It depends on the package and vehicle condition. A full detail can take between 4–8 hours.

Do I need to book an appointment?

Yes. We recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred date and time.

Do you offer mobile detailing services?

Currently, all services are done at our shop to ensure high-quality results.

How long does a wrap or paint job last?

With proper care, wraps can last 3–5 years and paint jobs even longer. We provide maintenance tips with every job.

Can I bring my own parts or materials?

Absolutely. Just let us know in advance, and we’ll let you know if it meets our standards.

What’s your cancellation policy?

Please cancel at least 24 hours in advance. Late cancellations may incur a fee.
